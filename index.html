Staking

Locked staking designed for patient holders.

Umoja V2 staking will launch with fixed-term pools, aligned with the current 3-month global sell lock. The goal is simple: reward the people who believe and hold.

90 days – Base APR 180 days – Higher APR 365 days – Long-term believers
Staking UI & contracts in preparation – will be announced publicly before deployment.

How staking will work

  1. Connect wallet on the official Umoja Staking portal.
  2. Select lock period (90 / 180 / 365 days) and amount to stake.
  3. Confirm the transaction and receive a staking receipt.
  4. Rewards accrue on-chain; users can claim after lock expires.
  5. Everything will be verifiable and auditable on BNB Chain.

Final APRs and smart-contract addresses will be shared with the community before launch. Do not interact with any contract that is not announced on the official channels.
